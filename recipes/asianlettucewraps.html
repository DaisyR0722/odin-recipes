Asian Lettuce Wraps

Description

Lettuce leaves with a scoop of beef mixture in the center

Ingredients

  • 16 Boston Bibb or butter lettuce leaves
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup hoisin sauce
  • 2 cloves fresh garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice wine vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons minced pickled ginger
  • 1 dash Asian chile pepper sauce (Optional)
  • 1 (8 Ounce) can water chestnuts, drained and finely chopped
  • 1 bunch green onions, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons Asian (dark) sesame oil

Steps

  1. Rinse lettuce leaves and pat dry, careful not to tear them. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet on medium-high heat. Add beef, cook and stir for 5 to 7 minutes until browned and crumbly. Drain and discard grease. Transfer beef to a bowl. Add onion to same skillet. Cook and stir for 5 to 7 minutes until just tender.
  3. Stir in hoisin sauce, garlic, soy sauce, vinegar, ginger, and chile pepper sauce until well combined. Add water chestnuts, green onions, sesame oil, and cooked beef. Cook and stir for 2 minutes until the onions just begin to wilt.
  4. Arrange lettuce leaves around the outer edge of a large serving platter. Place beef mixture in the center of each lettuce leaf.
